Generator Ranges & Typical Applications

2–10 kVA
Homes, kiosks, small offices. Compact portable and inverter-compatible units available.
15–50 kVA
Retail, schools, small clinics, light workshops. Silent canopies available for urban sites.
60–150 kVA
Hospitals, data closets, restaurants and medium industries — reliable standby and prime configurations.
200–500 kVA
Large hotels, factories and commercial buildings — containerised and canopy options.
600–2000 kVA
Industrial parks, mining, telecom hubs — multi-set paralleling solutions and turnkey installations.
Hybrid Systems
Integrate diesel with batteries and solar for reduced fuel costs and improved runtime during extended outages.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we install a generator?
Small units can be installed in days. Larger containerised systems depend on site readiness and logistics — we provide a clear schedule during the proposal stage.
Do you offer remote monitoring?
Yes. EmersonEIMS supplies telemetry and performance dashboards to track runtime, fuel levels and alerts.
